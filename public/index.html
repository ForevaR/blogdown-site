Welcome to the f.R- blog, which is short for forever rising. It is a life mantra I coined for myself some time ago and originated by combining select words from two song titles in my Spotify library. Synonomous with always striving, bettering or continuously learning, it is my way forward; a personal compass of sorts. Hopefully, this blog will reflect my growth across the numerous interests I have. I aim to continue in the style of my old blog while also including some off-hand calculations, analysis, projects and other random tidbits I enjoy outside of research.
